About Us

Learn about our mission to reduce food waste and fight hunger

Our Story

FoodShare was founded in 2020 with a simple but powerful idea: to create a platform that connects those with surplus food to those who need it most.

The idea came when our founder, Sarah Johnson, witnessed the shocking amount of perfectly good food being thrown away at a local restaurant at the end of the day. At the same time, just a few blocks away, a community shelter was struggling to provide enough meals for those in need.

Sarah realized that the problem wasn't a lack of food, but rather a lack of connection between those who had excess and those who needed it. With a background in technology and a passion for community service, she assembled a team of like-minded individuals to create FoodShare.

What started as a small local initiative has now grown into a nationwide platform that has facilitated the donation of over 10,000 meals and prevented thousands of pounds of food from going to waste.

Our Mission

To reduce food waste and alleviate hunger by creating efficient connections between food donors and recipients, ensuring that surplus food reaches those who need it most.

Our Vision

A world where no edible food goes to waste and no one goes hungry, fostering sustainable communities through the equitable distribution of food resources.
