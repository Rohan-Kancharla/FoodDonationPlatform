Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters related to food donations, please call our donation hotline at (123) 456-7891.

How can I volunteer with FoodShare?

We welcome volunteers for various roles including food pickup/delivery, event coordination, and administrative support. Please select "Volunteer Opportunities" in the contact form above, and our volunteer coordinator will reach out with current opportunities.

Can I schedule a tour of your facility?

Yes, we offer tours of our main facility by appointment. Please contact us using the form above or call our main office to schedule a visit. Tours are typically available on Tuesdays and Thursdays between 10am and 2pm.
